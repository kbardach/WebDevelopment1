About us

Our painting company, founded in the early 1900s, has been passed down through generations, embodying a rich tradition of craftsmanship and dedication. Today, we are a team of four skilled professionals committed to delivering high-quality painting services. We cater to clients in Stockholm and the surrounding areas, ensuring that each project we undertake is completed with the utmost precision and care. Our legacy and small, dedicated team allow us to offer personalized service and exceptional results for every client. Over the years, we've honed our expertise, blending traditional techniques with modern innovations to meet the evolving needs of our customers. From residential to commercial projects, our attention to detail and commitment to excellence remain unwavering. We take pride in transforming spaces and bringing our clients' visions to life. Our long-standing reputation in the community is built on trust, quality workmanship, and a passion for the art of painting.
